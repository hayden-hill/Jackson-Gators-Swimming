Our Goal

Our goal here in Jackson is to provide a friendly yet competitive environment for our swimmers. We strive to help your child succeed in their goals in swimming and to also teach them certain morals to carry on throughout life. We want to teach discipline and hard work and to show them the benefits that these skills can give them later on in life.


You can use the two page windows below to see your swimmer's times in high school and how they compare to colleges. We use this so that we can see how we rank in colleges and to show us how we did at our High School State Championship meet. The two windows show the times for the male swimmers on one and on the other the female swimmers times for the swimmers at High School State.
