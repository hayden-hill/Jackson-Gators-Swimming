Where are we?

We practice at The University of Memphis Lambuth. The campus has a fairly nice pool equipped with Lane Ropes and Blocks. We try to keep the chemicals and the temperature stable. There are both women and men locker rooms, as well as a family locker room. For spectators, there are bleachers overlooking the pool on the building's second story.
